How Scrappy Works

A simple flow from signup to sale. Follow these steps to get your first order.

  1. 1

    Create your account

    Sign up with your email or Google. Choose Buyer, Seller, or both. Verify your email — you're ready to start.

    Sign up
  2. 2

    Set up your profile

    Add a photo, short bio, and your preferred location. Sellers can connect Stripe for payouts and set shipping regions.

    Profile settings
  3. 3

    List your first product

    Upload product photos, set your price, and add tags. A good title and description help buyers find you faster.

    Create listing

    Your products appear in Scrappy’s marketplace and curated feeds. Interact with buyers and boost your visibility.

    Explore marketplace

    Buyers complete their purchase through our secure Stripe checkout. You receive payments automatically — minus platform fees.
